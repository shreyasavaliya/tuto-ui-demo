Key findings

Inflation protection is working. Your 3% compound rider has grown your original $150/day benefit to $247/day — a 65% increase since your policy was issued in 2005.
The rate increase is above average, but within historical norms for your carrier and issue year. Genworth policies from 2000–2008 have seen average cumulative increases of 60–80%.
Your claim probability is meaningful. A 67-year-old woman has a 58% lifetime probability of needing at least 90 days of care — higher than most people assume.
Daily benefit may lag care costs. Current median home care in California is ~$270/day. Your benefit covers about 92% of that today, decreasing over time without further increases.
